Peters Financial Consulting

We are a Wisconsin-based financial consulting company that assists individuals and entities who have sustained a financial loss due to some insured event or litigated dispute.

Services

We provide in-depth, personalized accounting services to both businesses and individuals.

Lost Income Evaluation

Our team delves deeply into the data provided to conduct an object analysis of economic damages.

Funds Flow Analysis

Our analysis covers cash inflows and outflows, as well as various other financial assets.

Damaged Property Valuation

We tackle incidents involving damage to personal property through another's negligence, willful destruction, or natural disaster.

Business Valuation

We examine a variety of economic factors pertinent to the business or an owner's interest in a company.

Construction Evaluation

We meticulously sift through all applicable data to provide a complete cost analysis for affected property.

Subrogation Analysis

We work with insurance providers and law offices to provide financial and forensic analyses relating to damages.
